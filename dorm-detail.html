Overview

Park View Residence is a modern dormitory popular with ABAC students, offering fully furnished rooms, strong Wi-Fi, and 24/7 security. Located within walking distance from campus and close to cafés, convenience stores, and food stalls.

Facilities

  • Air-conditioned rooms with private bathroom
  • Free Wi-Fi in all rooms and common areas
  • Keycard access and 24/7 security guard
  • Laundry machines and water vending machines
  • Motorbike and car parking area
