About ABAC Hub

ABAC Hub is an unofficial student-made concept inspired by websites like ABACToday. It is designed as a centralized platform for ABAC students to access campus news, study guides, and real student reviews about courses and teachers.

Vision

To make university life easier by putting the most important information in one place and presenting it in a clear, student-friendly way.

Key Features in this Prototype

  • Home page with featured article and latest news.
  • News & articles section with filters and search UI.
  • Course & teacher review listing and detail page.
  • Login screen UI for future authentication.

Future Ideas

  • Real login and account system for ABAC students.
  • GPA calculator and exam planner.
  • Student marketplace for books and dorm items.
  • Comment system on articles and discussion boards.
Note: This is a frontend-only prototype for study and presentation purposes. It is not an official university website.
