Course Overview

Business Statistics introduces students to descriptive and inferential statistics commonly used in business and management. Topics include data visualization, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, and simple regression.

Summary

  • Weekly lectures with practical examples.
  • Regular quizzes based on lecture notes and slides.
  • Midterm and final exam with both calculation and theory.
  • Useful for marketing, finance, and data-related careers.
